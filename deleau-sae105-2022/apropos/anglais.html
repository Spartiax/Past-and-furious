About us

Our food truck is called the « Past and furious », as you will surely understed this truck refers to the saga that is loved so much. Our food truck will propose you meals based on pasta which are easy and fast to eat.

Our story

The idea of creating this food truck crossed our minds years ago, but the budget, the implementation of one of the biggest project of our life was complicated. But we succeeded and today the past and furious is here and we will be happy to make the fastest food you will ever see.

What we do

The principe of our food truck is to make you eat the best pasta you have ever eat before while having a spirit of conviviality and speed. Obviously, we only make fresh pasta and we use the maximum of products from our local producers. Our products are all fresh and the dishes will be prepared in front of you. There will be several types of pasta and different tastes to satisfy everyone’s needs, like our dish that has been thought for vegans.
